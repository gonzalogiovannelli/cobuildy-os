# Cobuildy — Company Profile

## Mission
Structure and connect real estate projects with the right capital.

## Value Proposition
Cobuildy is a capital partner for real estate developers in Spain and Portugal.
We take development projects from idea to funded — structuring the deal,
preparing investor-ready materials, and connecting with the right investors.
We only charge a success fee. No upfront cost to the developer.

## What we do
1. **Structure** — deal structuring and financial modeling
2. **Prepare** — pitch decks, data rooms, documentation
3. **Outreach** — direct access to 100+ professional investors
4. **Close** — coordinate all parties until capital is secured

## What we are NOT
- We are not a fund. We do not pool investor capital.
- We do not provide financial or legal advice.
- We act as an introducer between developers and investors.

## Business model
- Success fee only — we get paid when capital is raised
- Minimum developer skin in the game: 10% equity
- Land must be secured before we take on a project
- Ticket range: €600K — €2.5M+

## Markets
- Spain (primary)
- Portugal (active)

## Financing instruments
- Senior loan
- Private equity
- Bridge loan
- Mezzanine
